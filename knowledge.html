Standard Operating Procedures

Tomstudio Biz Hub is a multi-service technology and business support center. These SOPs standardize operations across all hubs.

1. Tech Academy SOP

Purpose: Deliver quality tech education consistently.

Responsible: Academy Lead/Instructor/Admin

Tools: Registration form, timetable, receipt book, LMS/Telegram, attendance sheet

Procedure:
  1. Greet student/parent and explain courses
  2. Share schedule, duration, and fees
  3. Register student details
  4. Collect payment and issue receipt
  5. Add student to class group (Telegram/WhatsApp/LMS)
  6. Conduct classes as scheduled
  7. Take attendance and assign tasks
  8. Track progress and assessments
  9. Issue certificates at completion

Outcome: Enrolled students complete training successfully.

2. Printing & Graphics Hub SOP

Purpose: Handle design and print jobs efficiently and profitably.

Responsible: Printing Operator/Designer/Admin

Tools: Design software, printers, job card, price list

Procedure:
  1. Receive job request
  2. Confirm size, quantity, material, deadline
  3. Provide price and timeline
  4. Collect advance/full payment
  5. Prepare design or confirm client artwork
  6. Print test/sample if required
  7. Complete production
  8. Quality check
  9. Deliver job
  10. Record job and payment
Design Responsibilities:
  • Create monthly "Happy New Month" designs
  • Create weekly "Happy New Week" designs
  • Design festive/seasonal greetings
  • Produce promotional e-flyers
  • Ensure all designs follow brand guidelines

3. Photography Hub SOP

Purpose: Provide professional photography services.

Responsible: Photographer/Editor/Admin

Tools: Camera, memory cards, editing software, contract form

Procedure:
  1. Receive booking request
  2. Confirm date, location, package
  3. Collect booking fee
  4. Prepare equipment
  5. Cover event/session
  6. Backup files immediately
  7. Edit selected photos
  8. Deliver within agreed timeline
  9. Request feedback/referral

Outcome: Satisfied clients and referrals.

4. Gadget Hub SOP

Purpose: Sell gadgets profitably with accountability.

Responsible: Sales Officer/Storekeeper

Tools: Inventory list, receipt book, price list

Procedure:
  1. Receive customer inquiry
  2. Explain product features and price
  3. Confirm availability
  4. Collect payment
  5. Issue receipt
  6. Release product
  7. Update inventory
  8. Offer after-sales support

Outcome: Accurate sales and stock records.

5. Tech Lab SOP

Purpose: Provide repairs, maintenance, and tech support.

Responsible: Technician/Admin

Tools: Diagnostic tools, job card, spare parts

Procedure:
  1. Receive faulty device
  2. Diagnose issue
  3. Inform client of cost and timeline
  4. Collect diagnostic/advance fee
  5. Repair device
  6. Test functionality
  7. Deliver device
  8. Collect balance payment
  9. Record job details

Outcome: Functional devices and repeat clients.

6. CBT/ICT Skills SOP

Purpose: Train candidates for computer-based tests and ICT basics.

Responsible: CBT Supervisor/Instructor

Tools: Computers, CBT software, attendance sheet

Procedure:
  1. Register candidates
  2. Collect training fee
  3. Assign system/login
  4. Conduct CBT practice sessions
  5. Monitor progress
  6. Provide feedback
  7. Conduct mock tests
  8. Issue completion note

Outcome: Confident and exam-ready candidates.

7. Admin & Finance SOP

Purpose: Ensure transparency and financial stability.

Responsible: Admin Officer/Manager

Reporting Structure:
  1. All hub staff report to Hub Admin Officer
  2. Admin Officer reports to Hub Manager within 24 hours
  3. Only Hub Manager/Director may approve refunds, credit jobs, major expenses
  4. Unresolved issues must not remain pending beyond 48 hours
Procedure:
  1. Record all income and expenses daily
  2. Reconcile cash and transfers
  3. Submit weekly report
  4. Conduct monthly review
  5. Save records securely

8. Customer Service SOP

Purpose: Maintain strong brand reputation

Procedure:
  1. Greet customers politely
  2. Listen actively
  3. Offer clear solutions
  4. Avoid arguments
  5. Escalate unresolved issues
  6. Follow up after service

Outcome: Happy customers and referrals.

Apprentice Guidelines

Purpose: Define conduct, responsibilities, and learning expectations.

Responsibilities:
  • Report punctually according to schedule
  • Maintain professional conduct
  • Follow supervisor instructions
  • Handle tools responsibly
  • Keep work areas neat
  • Maintain confidentiality
Task Engagement:
  • Assist on live jobs only under supervision
  • Must not collect payments unless authorized
  • Must not quote prices or promise timelines
  • Responsibilities increase based on competence
Attendance & Discipline:
  • Regular attendance is mandatory
  • Absence must be communicated in advance
  • Persistent lateness may lead to termination

"Consistency builds trust. Trust builds growth."

All staff must understand and follow these SOPs strictly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I reset my password?

A: Contact the admin to reset your password. Admin can reset from the Admin Panel.

Q: Can I edit a job after creation?

A: Yes, but only before it moves to the next stage. Once a job is routed, contact admin for changes.

Q: How do I generate an invoice?

A: Invoices are automatically generated when a job is created. Click the invoice icon on any job to view/download.

Q: How do I contact support?

A: Call +234 803 082 7417 or email support@tbos.com

Q: What payment methods are accepted?

A: We accept cash, bank transfer, Paystack, and Flutterwave payments.

Q: How long does job delivery take?

A: Delivery time depends on the job type. Standard jobs take 2-3 days. Complex jobs may take longer.

Q: Can clients track their jobs?

A: Yes! Clients can use the Client Portal to track job status using their Tracking ID or Phone Number.

Q: How do I upload files for my job?

A: Use the Client Portal file upload section or send files via WhatsApp to +234 803 082 7417
